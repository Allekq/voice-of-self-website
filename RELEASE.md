# Release Scope

This file is the source of truth for the current public launch scope and the
minimum legal and operational checks to revisit before expanding release
regions.

## Current Public Launch Scope

Public App Store availability is currently intended to include:

- Austria
- Belgium
- Bulgaria
- Croatia
- Cyprus
- Czech Republic
- Denmark
- Estonia
- Finland
- France
- Germany
- Greece
- Hungary
- Ireland
- Italy
- Latvia
- Lithuania
- Luxembourg
- Malta
- Netherlands
- Poland
- Portugal
- Romania
- Slovakia
- Slovenia
- Spain
- Sweden
- United Kingdom

Do not enable "Make your app automatically available in all future App Store
countries or regions."

## Not In Public Launch Yet

These regions are intentionally postponed for now:

- United States
- Canada
- Switzerland
- Iceland
- Norway
- Africa, Middle East, and India
- Latin America and the Caribbean
- Asia Pacific
- Non-EU European countries not listed above

The current reason is scope control, not product readiness. The goal is to
stay within a manageable legal and support footprint.

## TestFlight Exception

Friends and trusted testers in countries outside the public launch scope may
still use TestFlight.

Current expectation:

- US friends can be invited through TestFlight
- Canadian friends can be invited through TestFlight

Do not treat TestFlight distribution as approval to expand public App Store
availability without revisiting the checks below.

## App Store Localizations

Keep metadata localizations limited to:

- English (United States)
- Polish

Optional:

- English (United Kingdom), only if there is a real need for UK-specific store
  copy or screenshots

Do not add extra App Store metadata localizations unless the listing,
screenshots, support flow, and legal copy are ready for them.

## Before Expanding Beyond EU Plus UK

Before adding the US, Canada, Switzerland, or any other new region, recheck:

- privacy policy accuracy
- terms accuracy
- app country availability
- subscription and in-app purchase availability
- support capacity
- whether a public contact address, business address, or phone number needs to
  be disclosed for that market
- whether any region-specific rights or disclosures need to be added

If product behavior changes what data is collected, stored, deleted, exported,
shared, or processed by AI providers, update the legal documents as part of the
same change.
